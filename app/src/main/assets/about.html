How to use

There are a few questions to test your knowledge on capital cities of the world. To begin, press the "Take the Quiz!" button. Each question is multiple choice with 5 possible options. You can navigate between the questions and press "Submit Answers" when you are ready to submit! After submitting, you will get a results screen. Read it carefully, you need to retake the quiz to see it again!

Features

  • Translated in both French and English
  • Difficult selection of questions.
  • Answers are automatically saved for if you want to finish the quiz later.
